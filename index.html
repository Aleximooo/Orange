About

My name is Alex Wright, and I’m a musician hailing from Orange, Connecticut. I’m a cellist and producer with an interest in session work, sound design, and composition.
I attended Berklee as a cellist, where I earned the Berklee Contemporary Symphony Orchestra award under the guidance of Julius P. Williams. I’ve also played for various student projects under the Screen Scoring Program, and in various club performances. I studied under the Electronic Production & Design Major and graduated in 2026.
I worked with music technology, and worked with programs like Ableton Live, ProTools, MaxforLive, and VS Code. I hope to become a working professional on both sides of the recording booth, and would like to work in audio engineering.
When I work, I’d love to produce and write for myself and other artists. I would also love to teach younger musicians. You can find some of my production work on the right!
