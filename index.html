About

My name is Alex Wright, and I’m a musician hailing from Orange, Connecticut. I’m a cellist and producer with an interest in session work, sound design, and composition. I attended Berklee as a cellist, where I earned the Berklee Contemporary Symphony Orchestra award under the guidance of Julius P. Williams. I’ve also played for various student projects under the Screen Scoring Program, and in various club performances. I studied under the Electronic Production & Design Major.
In studying my major, I worked with music tech and sound design/engineering, and became ingrained with programs such as Ableton Live, ProTools, MaxforLive, and VS Code. I hope to become a working professional on both sides of the recording booth, and would like to explore more of the audio engineering field. Additionally, I’d like to produce and compose my own works, professionally.
You can contact me at aawright03@gmail.com and write to @alwrighty03 on instagram for inquiries.
